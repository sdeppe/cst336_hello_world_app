What Are They?

While Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality seem rather different at first glance, there share many similarities. One way to tell them apart would be to think of Augmented Reality like a version of VR that is based off the real world. While VR is used to create a virtual world, AR is used to create artificial objects in the real world.
